Operation: Hotel Raid

Can You Catch Them All Before It’s Too Late?

Daring Heist: Hotel Raid is an exciting detective-style game where you play as a sharp-witted police officer tracking down five elusive thieves hiding in a mysterious 15-room hotel. Each thief is hidden in a separate room, and it’s up to you to raid the right ones, follow clues, and bring them to justice. Think fast, raid smart — the city is counting on you!
